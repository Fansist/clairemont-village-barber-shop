Our Story

A true Clairemont institution

Classic neighborhood barbershop in the Clairemont Village shopping center, in business for roughly 55 years.

We specialize in haircuts for men and boys, traditional cuts, tapers, and clipper work at fair prices. This is a true walk-in, community shop, and plenty of our regulars have been coming back for decades. Open seven days a week, Monday through Friday 9am, 6pm and weekends until 5pm.
